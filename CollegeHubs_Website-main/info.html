Dodge Gaming Club

It's fun to work the gaming arm in part because it's really a team effort.

Dodge Gaming Club at NIET is to create a dynamic and inclusive community that fosters a passion for competitive and recreational gaming among our students. It's a platform for all the gamers who left their passion to be in the mainstream to gear their skills and explore the opportunities.

Yoga Club

The college Yoga club is a community of students passionate about promoting physical and mental well-being through yoga. It offers classes for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced, and provides opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals. Joining the club is a great way to enhance your yoga practice and experience the benefits of a regular yoga practice.

Nritya Bhakti (Traditional Dance Club)

Nrityabhakti (a traditional dance club of NIET) as the name suggests! We as a club is not only a dance club , we are here to celebrate our diversity and promote cultural appreciation. We have dedicated ourselves to learn and perform a wide range of traditional dances from various regions and cultures. From graceful classical dances to energetic folk dances to bollywood ,we have explored the rich tapestry of our traditions.

Kathputliyaan (The Theatre Club)

Kathputliyaan, a name that resonates a certain enthusiasm and an undying passion for theater. A society where adrenaline rushes, creative experiments and a smell of bravado resides, eagerly offers a platform to tell various genres of stories through theater plays, nukkad nataks and films. A space of our college where the exhalation of emotions is received by the shining eyes of the audience. We work on the belief of collecting fragments from small acquaintances in life, as the world's a stage for us.
